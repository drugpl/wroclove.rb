Tips
01
How to write my bio?
Tell us a bit about yourself - what excites you professionally and what you're currently working on. What drives you in the Ruby community, and what accomplishments or interests in software engineering make you proud?

Feel free to share a glimpse of your life outside of code, too. Anything that helps us get to know you better is welcome.
02
Elevator pitch
Capture the essence of your talk in just a few concise sentences - what it's about, why it matters, and what the audience will gain from it. Highlight the key takeaways, whether it's an exciting new tool or a smart way to tackle a tricky problem.

Your goal is simple: make us genuinely excited to attend!
