What we offer
Company visibility
We will publish information about you being a supporter on our social media. Your company will be visible on post-conference videos, in the conference room between the talks and on our website in the supporters section, alongside with a link. We periodically send out a newsletter to our subscribers — we’ll include your company logo there as well!
Communication channel with participants
You will have access to our Discord server, where participants can engage with your company representatives. Note that being present on Discord is not mandatory so likely not all participants will be there, although we will encourage it.
Tickets
Buying Supporter Ticket already grants permission to enter the conference for 1 person, with a distinctive badge, which may help with networking :) Additionally, if tickets run out we guarantee an option to buy three additional tickets outside the regular ticket batches.
Single package
At wroclove.rb, we've always kept things simple - focusing on great speakers, meaningful conversations, and bringing Ruby developers together. With that spirit in mind, we offer a single supporter package priced at 800 EUR (net).

If you're looking for a way to give back to the Ruby community and connect with ~150 passionate participants, supporting our conference is a fantastic opportunity.
